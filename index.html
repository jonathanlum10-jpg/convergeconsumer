What is ConvergeCONSUMER?

ConvergeCONSUMER is Deloitte's AI-powered decision system for consumer businesses. It uses agentic AI and proprietary external data to replace how organizations make pricing, forecasting, inventory, segmentation, and growth decisions: across retail, grocery, CPG, restaurants, travel, and automotive. Each product is built around a specific business decision, with sector logic, external signals, and decision intelligence already encoded, then configured to your data and deployed in weeks.
